About ATTDF

Empowering Africa Through Technology and Innovation

Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) is an organization registered in accordance with the laws of the federal republic of Nigeria with the objective to promote Technology Transfer(Skill Transfer), Innovation, Investment, Industrialization, Health, Agricultural, Environmental, Infrastructural, Education and Human Capital Development for massive job creation. The organization is registered with Corporate Affairs Commission with the registration number 72078. From the inception of the establishment of the Organization in 2014, Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) has been developing high-knowledge based inventiveness geared towards fulfilling the objectives it was established, especially in the areas of technology transfer (skills transfer), educational, human capital development among others to enhance employability and career development in specialized fields for Nigerians, other Africans and persons from other nations of the world in various high saleable relevant fields/skills to fit into critical sectors of the economic ranging from Oil/Gas sector, Maritime and Aviation Sectors, Telecommunication and ICT Sector, Manufacturing Sector, Construction Sector, Renewable Energy Sector, International Organizations and Artisanship Services Sector etc. Worthy of note is that, the ATTDF has been involved in developing and execution of result- based programs and projects for purposes of enhancing investments and industrialization for job creation and sustainable economic growth and developments

MISSION:
Embarking on massive knowledge and skills transfer (Specialized Career Development) Programs in various relevant and sellable fields for employability enhancement and career growth along with programs and projects to boost investment, industrialization and overall economic growth and sustainable development in a favourable climate
VISION:
To promote relevant and needful programs / Projects that will contribute to actualization of sustainable economic growth and development to complement government effort in making Nigeria and other African nations economically very viable, especially in the areas of developing skilled and competent manpower and bridging gaps in critical sectors of economy as captured in the objectives of the organization

General Information about ATTDF

What is ATTDF?

The Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) is a Nigerian-registered organization focused on promoting technology and skill transfer, innovation, investment, and human capital development for economic growth and job creation.

When was ATTDF established?

ATTDF was founded in 2014 and has since been implementing high-knowledge-based programs across multiple sectors.

What are the main focus areas of ATTD

EKey focus areas include Technology Transfer, Education, Innovation, Health, Agriculture, Industrialization, Environment, Infrastructure, and Human Capital Development.
