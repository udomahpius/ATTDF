About ATTDF

Empowering Africa Through Technology and Innovation

Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) is an organization registered in accordance with the laws of the federal republic of Nigeria with the objective to promote Technology Transfer(Skill Transfer), Innovation, Investment, Industrialization, Health, Agricultural, Environmental, Infrastructural, Education and Human Capital Development for massive job creation. Read More

MISSION
Specialized Career Development

Embarking on massive knowledge and skills transfer Programs in various relevant and sellable fields for employability enhancement and career growth along with programs and projects to boost investment, industrialization and overall economic growth and sustainable development in a favourable climate. Read More

VISION
Empowering Africa’s Workforce

To promote relevant and needful programs / Projects that will contribute to actualization of sustainable economic growth and development to complement government effort in making Nigeria and other African nations economically very viable, especially in the areas of developing skilled and competent manpower and bridging gaps in critical sectors of economy as captured in the objectives of the organization.
