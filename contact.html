General Information about ATTDF

What is ATTDF?

The Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) is a Nigerian-registered organization focused on promoting technology and skill transfer, innovation, investment, and human capital development for economic growth and job creation.

When was ATTDF established?

ATTDF was founded in 2014 and has since been implementing high-knowledge-based programs across multiple sectors.

What are the main focus areas of ATTDF

EKey focus areas include Technology Transfer, Education, Innovation, Health, Agriculture, Industrialization, Environment, Infrastructure, and Human Capital Development.
