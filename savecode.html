Ongoing Programs for Career Growth

Empowering Skilled Careers Through Specialized Training & Talent Promotion From Industry-Focused Programs and Oil & Gas Business Insights to Showcasing Skilled Talent and Enhancing Employability Opportunities

Program Specialized Career Development Programs

ATTDF in collaboration with Lagos Aviation and Maritime Business Academy, runs specialized career programs focused on fields and skills relevant in the Oil and Gas, Maritime, Aviation, and other allied industries, including international organizations. These programs are structured to provide participants with practical, high-level industry knowledge that enhances competence and boosts employability. The training is also designed to support career advancement for professionals already working in various organizations. Areas of training include engineering, management, logistics, safety, health, security, renewable energy, and other fields tailored to the needs of individuals or organizations.

Oil and Gas Economic Rap Session

This ongoing program is a knowledge-sharing and empowerment session that helps participants discover how to position themselves as key players in the Oil and Gas value chain. It offers direction on exploring business opportunities in the sector and provides insights that can help individuals gain a strong foothold in the industry. The session is geared toward practical exposure and guidance to build economic relevance in a thriving sector

Skilled Manpower Data Collation Initiative

ATTDF is preparing to launch a manpower data collation platform that will feature skilled and competent individuals for internship and employment visibility. The initiative is aimed at promoting trained individuals who possess both industry-relevant certifications and personal development traits such as emotional intelligence, resilience, and interpersonal skills. Those who meet the eligibility criteria can have their information featured and marketed to interested organizations and employers

Employability Promotion and Partner Collaboration

While ATTDF does not function as a recruitment or outsourcing agency, it actively supports employability by giving visibility to skilled individuals and providing relevant data to interested companies and institutions. This value-driven initiative fosters job connections and promotes human capital development. Interested individuals are expected to submit a scanned partnership letter, photographs, and other relevant information to participate in this employability promotion drive.
